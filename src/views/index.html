Les plaintes d'un Icare

Directed by Leila Rami

Short-length (16/9)

7'40

2014

Role : Camera operator / Video editor

Les plaintes d'un Icare is the adaptation of the Greek myth of Icarus into our modern times. Icarus is a young neurotic man who inherits a large sum of money, which he squirts in social gatherings, where money, drugs and alcohol flow. In the attempt to reach his happiness, Icarus in the paroxysm of his delirium loose his life.
